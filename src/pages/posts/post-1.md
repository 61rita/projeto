description: 'This project aims to address the confusion and uncertainty surrounding UX Design job terminologies by creating a web platform that organizes and simplifies these terms. The platform helps users, particularly new and curious professionals, identify and understand different UX Design positions, enabling them to choose roles that align best with their skills and interests within the industry. The goal is to serve as a comprehensive guide and repository for navigating the diverse landscape of UX Design job titles and descriptions.'
